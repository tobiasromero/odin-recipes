Recipe:Pizza

description

This pizza in the best Italian style is one of the most recognized pizzas in the world, its aroma and flavor are very special It takes a very extensive leavening, which leads to a very particular and characteristic cornicione of this dish.

ingredients

  • flour 00 o 000: 800g
  • water 400g
  • lyeast 2g
  • oil of olive
  • salt 10g
  • sugar 5g
  • semolina

preparation

  1. In a container, place the water with the sugar and stir until dissolved
  2. place the yeast in the container and dissolve until it becomes uniform with the water
  3. with a spoon put 2 tablespoons of flour in the container with water and stir, repeat this step until there is 1/4 (by eye) of flour in the container
  4. place the salt together with the flour and continue adding spoonfuls and stirring until a dough is formed
  5. after the dough is uniform, remove it from the container and knead for 15 minutes
  6. put the dough in a container with a splash of olive oil and coat the dough with the oil
  7. cover the container with a bag and let it rest for 20 minutes
  8. Take out the dough and knead for another 15 minutes, put it back in the container and let it rest for 3 hours
  9. take out the dough and make 4 buns of equal weight, shape them so that they are smooth and round buns
  10. in a container or in different ones, put the buns with olive oil and let them rest for 3 hours
  11. once the time has passed, take out the already baked buns and one by one put them on a mountain of semolina to stretch them
  12. smear the dough with semolina and on the same mountain stretch the dough from the inside out, you have to assemble the cornicione on the sides
  13. transfer the already stretched dough to a baking dish and in a very hot oven cook the dough until it is almost cooked
  14. then take out the dough and add the tomato puree and mozzarella and put it back in the oven until the mozzarella melts
  15. optional: a splash of olive oil, add basil and tomatoes
  16. enjoy
