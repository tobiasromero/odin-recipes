recipe: omelette

description

This omelette combines the flavor of potato and egg perfectly, a very easy and quick dish from Spain to make.

ingredients

  • oil of olive: 1 botle
  • eggs: 6
  • potatoes: 3 bigs or 6 litles
  • salt

preparation

  1. Remove the skin from the potatoes and cut them into small cubes.
  2. In a frying pan, heat olive oil (the oil should cover the potatoes when placing them)
  3. Fry the potatoes until they are soft inside and a little crispy on the outside.
  4. Take out the potatoes and put them on paper so that the oil goes away.
  5. Cut a medium onion into julienne strips and fry them until soft.
  6. In a bowl, beat the eggs until they form bubbles.
  7. Place the potatoes and onion in the bowl and stir.
  8. Take a little oil from the pan and pour the mixture into the pan.
  9. wait for the mixture to cook on one side and turn until it cooks again
  10. serve and enjoy!
